Help

Hyperink an existing Dashboard to the selected Widget. This is useful to jump during presentations. Select the Dashboard from the left hand list, and then a Tab inside the Dashboard. Click Link to establish a new link, or Unlink to remove an existing link. Hyperlinks are shown in the title bar of a Widget with a -> icon. Click the X or press Escape to close this form.
