Use the buttons to show the bookmarks previously created. The bookmarks can link different Dashboards, which will show them one after another. Inside a Dashboard, bookmarks can optionally be created for one or more Widgets. These will be shown in the sequence of the Widget number (this can be changed by the user). Inside a Widget, different visualisations may be bookmarked. This allows for the display of different formats of the same graph, or even totally different graphs in the same Widget area.
