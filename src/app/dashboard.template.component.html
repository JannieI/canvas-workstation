A Template is a Dashboard that is displayed behind your Dashboard, for example your company name and logo. Templates cannot be edited here; it can be edited at any stage, similar to any other Dashboard. Changes to the template will reflect in all the Dashboards where it is used. Click the X or press Escape to close this form.

Double click a Dashboard in the grid to add it as a Template to the current Dashboard. Click Clear remove a previously linked Template.
